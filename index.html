HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Place as many pieces as possible on the board!
Fewer remaining squares = higher score.

PLACEMENT RULES

  • First piece must touch your corner
  • Next pieces must connect corner-to-corner with your own
  • Your pieces must never share an edge
  • Touching other players' edges is OK

CONTROLS

  • Select a piece from the panel below
  • ↻ Rotate / ↔ Flip to change orientation
  • Tap the board to place
  • ● Dots show valid positions

SCORING

Remaining squares × -1 point
All pieces placed = +15 bonus!
